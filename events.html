EMIRATES SHIPPING CONGRESS & IEC TELECOM DRIVE MARITIME DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION AT UAE MARITIME WEEK

Supported by the African Union Commission, United Nations, and Interpol, Africa Security Symposium (ASEC) 2025 hosts 300+ exhibitors and delegates to promote collaborations that improve the safety and security landscape of the continent. Taking place from June 10th to 12th, 2025 at Skylight Hotel in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, ASEC 2025 presents IEC Telecom with the ideal venue to showcase its satcom portfolio for critical communications.

IEC Telecom’s team – Najwa Ayoub (Sales Director) and Mark Hawkins (Senior Technology Consultant) – is keen to showcase its enhanced Starlink portfolio with resilient back-up options. IEC Telecom will feature OptiView (a network management platform that delivers real-time insights) as well as its bespoke tracking and PTT services. From reliable voice and data services to cybersecurity solutions, IEC Telecom supports the increasing demand for critical communication services in Africa with comprehensive network management solutions, tailormade airtime packages, and a 24/7 customer care team. In addition, during two in-depth sessions scheduled at ASEC 2025, IEC Telecom’s Mark Hawkins will take the conversation forward on building a dependable communications infrastructure as well as the challenges faced in a digital environment and the need for advanced cybersecurity solutions.

This exclusive pan-African event for government ministers, investors, and senior personnel from global enterprises schedules one-on-one meeting opportunities to facilitate long-term partnerships amongst key decision-makers. IEC Telecom is committed to ensuring the safety of critical mission personnel, the security of field assets, and the sustainability long-term operations.
